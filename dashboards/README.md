# Dashboards

This folder contains static screenshots of the Tableau dashboards created as part of the
Metrocar product analytics case.

The dashboards summarize key analytical findings and are intended to provide a quick,
visual overview of results without requiring access to Tableau or Tableau Public.

Each dashboard corresponds to a specific analytical focus and together they form a
complete analytical narrative.

---

## 1. Overview & Funnel Dashboard
**File:** `overview_funnel_dashboard.png`

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of overall product performance.

**What it shows:**
- Core product KPIs (completed rides, paid rides, revenue)
- User conversion funnel from app download to review
- Ride conversion funnel from request to payment
- Drop-off rates at each funnel stage

**Use case:**  
Quickly assess product health and identify the most critical funnel drop-off points.

---

## 2. Time-Based Operations Dashboard
**File:** `time_based_operations_dashboard.png`

This dashboard focuses on operational efficiency across time.

**What it shows:**
- Request → Accept conversion rate by weekday and hour
- Ride volume patterns throughout the day
- Average driver acceptance time by hour
- Cancellation rate trends over time

**Use case:**  
Identify time-based operational bottlenecks and demand–supply imbalances.

---

## 3. Platform & Age Performance Dashboard
**File:** `platform_age_performance_dashboard.png`

This dashboard analyzes performance across user segments.

**What it shows:**
- Platform contribution to completed rides
- Average revenue per ride by platform
- Completion rates by age group
- Completion and cancellation rates by age × platform

**Use case:**  
Understand how platform and demographic segments differ in performance and behavior.

---

## 4. Customer Ratings Dashboard
**File:** `customer_ratings_dashboard.png`

This dashboard focuses on customer satisfaction and feedback quality.

**What it shows:**
- Distribution of user ratings (1–5)
- Share of high, neutral, and low ratings
- Evidence of rating polarization
- Key qualitative insights derived from feedback patterns

**Use case:**  
Evaluate whether operational performance aligns with customer experience and satisfaction.
